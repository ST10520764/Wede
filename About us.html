Uthingo Ice

About Us

Uthingo Ice was established in 2023 by Nduna and Anne Madikane. Our mission is to supply fresh and affordable ice bags to businesses such as restaurants, clubs, and spaza shops. Competing with Ice2000, we aim to deliver quality and reliability to our customers.

We started Uthingo Ice with a simple belief; ice shouldnt be an afterthought. In a country where summer heat can be intense, where restaurants need consistent supply, and where loadshedding can disrupt daily operations, we saw the need for a dedicated, local ice supplier that never compromises on quality

What we do

We produce crystal-clear, odourless, and great-tasting ice using purified water and modern, food-grade equipment. Our ice is made in a hygienic facility, bagged securely, and stored in temperature-secured freezers so you always recieve a fresh, usable product. We know loadshedding is a reality. Thats why our facility runs on generator backup so production never runs stops, and your ice never melts before it reaches you.
